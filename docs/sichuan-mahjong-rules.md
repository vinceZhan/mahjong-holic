# Sichuan Mahjong Rules 🌶️🀄 (四川麻将)

Sichuan Mahjong is a fast-paced and highly interactive Mahjong variant popular in China.
It differs significantly from “classic” Mahjong in both **tile usage** and **game mechanics**.

---

## ⚡ Key Characteristics

* No Winds or Dragons 
* Mandatory **Ding Que (定缺)**
* Fast gameplay with **continuous winning**
* Emphasis on **simple patterns and speed**

---

## 🧩 Tile Set

### Used:

* Dots (Circles)
* Bamboo
* Characters (万)

### Removed:

* Winds (东南西北)
* Dragons (中发白)

👉 Total tiles: **108 tiles**

---

## 🪑 Players & Setup

* 4 players
* Each player starts with:

  * 13 tiles
* Dealer starts with:

  * 14 tiles

---

## 🎯 Objective

Same as standard Mahjong:

* Form **4 melds + 1 pair**
* Declare **Hu (胡)** to win

---

## 🔥 Core Rule: Ding Que (定缺)

At the beginning of the game:

1. Each player chooses **one suit to “lack”**

   * Dots / Bamboo / Characters
2. This suit is called your **Que (缺门)**

### ❗ Restrictions:

* You **cannot win** if you still have tiles from your chosen suit
* You should discard all tiles of that suit as early as possible

---

## 🔄 Gameplay Flow

Similar to basic Mahjong:

1. Draw a tile
2. Decide your move
3. Discard a tile

👉 BUT with one important priority:

* You must prioritize discarding your **Ding Que suit**

---

## 🔗 Meld Rules

* **Chow (吃)**: **NOT** allowed unless you are about to win
* **Pong (碰)**: allowed
* **Kong (杠)**: allowed

---

## 🏁 Winning (Hu / 胡)

You can win when:

* Your hand satisfies:

  * 4 melds + 1 pair (or 7 pairs)
* AND you have **no tiles from your Ding Que suit**

---

## 🌊 Special Mechanic: Blood Flow (血流成河)

This is what makes Sichuan Mahjong unique.

### 🔥 How it works:

* The game **does NOT stop** after one player wins
* Other players continue playing
* Multiple players can win in the same round

👉 This creates:

* More interaction
* More scoring opportunities
* Faster, more dynamic gameplay

---

## 🔁 Continuous Wins

* A player who wins stays in the round
* Others continue playing until:

  * All but one player have won, OR
  * Tiles run out

---

## 💰 Scoring (Simplified Overview)

Scoring rules vary, but commonly include:

* Base win 1 score (normal win from others)
* Get Fan (Doubles):
-- Self-draw: 1 Fan
-- Kong: 1 Fan
-- All pungs: 1 Fan
-- Full flush (having only one tile suit): 2 Fan
-- Seven pairs: 2 Fan
-- Win after Kong : 1 Fan
-- Robbing the Kong : 1 Fan
---

## 🧠 Strategy Tips

* Get rid of your **Ding Que suit early**
* Avoid holding mixed suits too long
* Watch opponents’ discards carefully

---

## 🔄 Comparison with Classic Mahjong

| Feature     | Sichuan Mahjong  | Classic Mahjong          |
| ----------- | ---------------- | ------------------------ |
| Honor Tiles | ❌ Removed        | ✅ Used                   |
| Ding Que    | ✅ Required       | ❌ Not used               |
| Game End    | Multiple winners | Usually ends after 1 win |
| Speed       | Fast             | Moderate                 |
